My Story

Hey there! I'm Neloy. Let's start with my undergraduate journey. During my academic years, I explored various areas of computer science. I worked on many projects, both individually and as part of a team, but my main focus has always been the AI sector. I kicked off my professional journey as an Associate Flutter Developer, working in this role for a few months. I then joined an apprenticeship, getting skilled in advanced machine learning, after which I joined a company as a Chatbot Developer. My specialization lies in AI tech stacks. I have a strong track record of developing both text- and voice-based conversational solutions. I've also created industry-standard automation products. Currently, I'm diving deeper into building advanced agentic workflow systems and n8n automation solutions.
